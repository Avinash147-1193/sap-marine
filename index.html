BULK COMMODITY DISTRIBUTION

Our company has been operating majorly in the following vessel types:

  • Panamax / Kamsarmax (66,000-95,000 DWT)
  • Supramax/Ultramax (50,000-64,000 DWT)
  • Handysize Segment (Up to 50,000 DWT)

From energy to agriculture, we handle a wide spectrum of dry bulk cargo with precision and scale. Coal leads our operations at 800,000 tonnes, followed by 500,000 tonnes of diverse bulk commodities and 300,000 tonnes of Iron Ore. We also expertly transport Grain (65,000 tonnes), Cement (50,000 tonnes), and Fertilizers (40,000 tonnes) - each requiring tailored logistics and care.

This distribution reflects our strength in managing complex supply chains across industries, reaffirming our commitment to safe, timely, and cost-effective cargo solutions.

Capt. Mukesh Verma

Director

Capt. Mukesh Verma is one of the founding members of the organisation. He graduated with a major in Nautical Sciences from Mumbai University in 1997, after spending a year at the Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad, studying Mining Engineering. Starting his career with KC Maritime, he sailed across the globe in dry bulk ships for 10+ years. He gained further experience at the Indian coast on coastal ships before finally hanging 4 stripes epaulette.

He took up commercial shipping as a broker and operator in 2009, prior to joining Avani Resources Pte Ltd in 2014. At Avani Resources, he headed the shipping team and managed Indonesia, South Africa, and the Mozambique regions for thermal coal and metallurgical coke from China, chartered more than 100 million tons in Handy, Smax, Pmax, and Capes to carry various energy and mineral commodities.
