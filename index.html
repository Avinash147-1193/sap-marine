Our Sustainability Approach

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At SAP MARINE INTERNATIONAL, we recognize the critical importance of sustainable operations in the maritime industry. Our approach to sustainability is comprehensive, addressing environmental, social, and governance aspects of our business.

We are committed to playing our part in the global transition to a more sustainable future, aligning our operations with international standards and best practices to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing our positive social impact.

Environmental Sustainability Initiatives

Fuel Efficiency & Carbon Emission Reduction

We implement various strategies to optimize fuel consumption and minimize carbon emissions from our shipping operations:

  • Advanced voyage planning to optimize routes and minimize fuel consumption
  • Speed optimization to balance operational efficiency with fuel economy
  • Regular monitoring and analysis of vessel performance to identify improvement opportunities
  • Close collaboration with vessel owners to implement energy-saving technologies
Eco-Friendly Ship Designs & Technology

We advocate for and work with vessel owners to adopt technologies that enhance environmental performance:

  • Hull optimization for improved hydrodynamic efficiency
  • Advanced propulsion systems that reduce fuel consumption
  • Exhaust gas cleaning systems to reduce air pollution
  • Use of alternative fuels where feasible
Ballast Water Management Systems

We ensure compliance with ballast water management regulations to prevent the transfer of invasive aquatic species:

  • Implementation of approved ballast water treatment systems
  • Regular monitoring and testing of ballast water
  • Proper record-keeping and reporting of ballast water operations
  • Training of crew on proper ballast water management procedures

Governance and Reporting

Our sustainability initiatives are integrated into our governance structure, with regular monitoring, evaluation, and reporting to ensure accountability and continuous improvement.

We adhere to international standards and frameworks for sustainability reporting, providing transparent communication of our environmental and social performance to stakeholders.

Capt. Mukesh Verma

Director

Capt. Mukesh Verma is one of the founding members of the organisation. He graduated with a major in Nautical Sciences from Mumbai University in 1997, after spending a year at the Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad, studying Mining Engineering. Starting his career with KC Maritime, he sailed across the globe in dry bulk ships for 10+ years. He gained further experience at the Indian coast on coastal ships before finally hanging 4 stripes epaulette.

He took up commercial shipping as a broker and operator in 2009, prior to joining Avani Resources Pte Ltd in 2014. At Avani Resources, he headed the shipping team and managed Indonesia, South Africa, and the Mozambique regions for thermal coal and metallurgical coke from China, chartered more than 100 million tons in Handy, Smax, Pmax, and Capes to carry various energy and mineral commodities.
